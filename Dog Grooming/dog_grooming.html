Essential Grooming Tips

Regular Brushing

Brush your dog 2-3 times per week to prevent matting and reduce shedding. Use appropriate brushes for your dog's coat type.

Nail Care

Trim nails every 2-4 weeks. Be careful not to cut the quick, and use proper nail clippers or grinders designed for dogs.

Bathing Schedule

Bathe your dog every 4-6 weeks using dog-specific shampoos. Over-bathing can strip natural oils from their coat.

Dental Care

Regular teeth brushing and dental chews help prevent dental disease and freshen breath.
